About Us

Sober Mind Service-Liberia (SMS- Liberian) is a non-profit organisation established to assit teenagers, youth, and young adults develop and maintain a normal way of thinking and behaving. This organisation was given birth to November 10, 2021 by Rev. Brandy D. Wolova, a Liberian humanitarian and truama facilitator.


SMS - Liberia's programs are designed to primarily alleviate and minimize the outcry of parents, guardians, and governments over the destruction of the souls or minds and destinies of the their children and wayward cititzens who are being affected daily by negative behaiviors and hobbies: lack of self-esteem, the use of drugs or substance abuse and other social vices.
